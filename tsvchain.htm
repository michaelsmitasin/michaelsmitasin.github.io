What is TSV Chain?


It's like CSVCHAIN but instead of echo'ing the next NFT to the end of a CSV, it captures the bits from passive optical taps, analyzes them with Zeek, and writes them to a TSV log file. This ensures chain-of-custody.
For more information: https://docs.zeek.org/en/master/log-formats.html?highlight=tsv#zeek-tsv-format-logs
